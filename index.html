GDG on Campus Delta University

Our Purpose

At GDG on Campus Delta University, our purpose is to foster a community of tech enthusiasts, developers, and innovators. We aim to provide a platform for learning, collaboration, and growth in the field of technology, with a focus on Google developer technologies.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students at University to become leaders in technology. We strive to create an inclusive environment where ideas flourish, skills are honed, and connections are made. Through workshops, hackathons, and networking events, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.
